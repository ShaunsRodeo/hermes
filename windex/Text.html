Push-Ups
Push-Ups are a bodyweight exercise that strengthen the chest, arms, and shoulders. Start in a plank position with your hands placed slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower your body down until your chest touches the ground and then push back up to the starting position. Repeat for desired reps.
Squats
Squats are a strength training exercise that work the legs, glutes, and hamstrings. Start with your feet hip-width apart and your hands in front of you. Lower your body down as if you were sitting back into a chair. Push back up to the starting position. Repeat for desired reps.
Plank
Plank is a bodyweight exercise that works the abs and core. Start in a push-up position, but instead of lowering your body down, hold the position for a set amount of time. Keep your body in a straight line and your abs engaged throughout the entire exercise.
